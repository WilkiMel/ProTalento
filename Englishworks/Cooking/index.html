Peperoni Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Tomato
  • Pizza dough
  • Mozarrella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Grated cheese
  • Oregano

Recipe:

  1. First, spread a layer of tomato paste on the dough using a spatula.
  2. Then, we place a layer of mozzarella.
  3. We cut 20 slices of pepperoni and distribute them evenly.
  4. Finally, we sprinkle grated cheese and oregano to taste.
  5. We bake for 10 to 15 minutes at 180ÂºC. It's ready!
